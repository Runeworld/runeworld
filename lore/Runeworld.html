Runeworld

Created by Kinshiki

A world with two continents known as the new and the old world. The old world is torn by political, religious, ethnic, and ideological conflicts while the new world is holding lost technologies and knowledge from ancient times. Master artisans utilize mysterious properties of different materials to craft objects that can manipulate the very building blocks of reality in a process called runecrafting. And an unlikely hero, who does not belong.
